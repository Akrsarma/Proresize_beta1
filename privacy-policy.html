Privacy Policy

Your images stay under your control.

This policy explains what ProResize does with your images, what information may be processed, and how privacy is handled.

Effective Date

Privacy-first image tools.

Effective date: March 27, 2026

ProResize is designed to provide simple image resizing, compression, and cropping tools while keeping user privacy as a core priority. This privacy policy explains how ProResize handles user data when you use the website.

1. Information ProResize processes

When you use ProResize, the images you choose are processed in your browser so the tool can resize, compress, crop, or optimize them. In general use, ProResize is designed so your images do not need to be uploaded to external servers for core functionality.

ProResize does not require you to create an account or provide personal details such as your name, address, age, or identification number in order to use the image tools.

2. How images are handled

ProResize aims to process selected images locally on your device within the browser environment. This means image editing actions are intended to happen on-device and not through remote storage for the normal operation of the tool.

You remain responsible for the images you choose to process and for how you use the exported files after download.

3. Personal data collection

ProResize does not intentionally collect personally identifiable information through its image processing workflow. The tool is built to be used without sign-up and without collecting sensitive personal profile data from users.

If you contact ProResize by email, the information you include in that message may be used only to respond to your request or provide support.

4. Cookies and local storage

ProResize may use simple browser storage features, such as local storage, to remember interface preferences like the selected theme. This helps improve usability, but it is not intended for tracking users across websites.

5. Third-party services

ProResize may load third-party resources that help present the website, such as hosted fonts or other standard web assets. Those providers may handle technical request data according to their own privacy practices.

6. Data security

ProResize is designed to minimize unnecessary data exposure by keeping core image processing in the browser. While reasonable care is taken in the design of the website, no internet-based service can guarantee absolute security in every situation.

7. Children's privacy

ProResize is a general-use utility and is not specifically directed toward children. It does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to this policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time to reflect feature changes, legal requirements, or service improvements. Any updates will be published on this page with a revised effective date.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or how ProResize handles privacy, you can contact: support@proresize.com
